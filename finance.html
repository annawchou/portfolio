Product Information

Yahoo Finance is one of the top 3 financial sites on the web with over 5 million unique users each day.

I am lead UI/UX designer for the Yahoo finance native iOS and android platforms. I am also lead designer for watchlist, portfolios, and holdings interactions on desktop and lead UX for mobile web.

The primary product goal for desktop was to allow users to find and discover information about the stock market. For our native apps, our goal was to allow users to have an app that saved their holdings/shares information and watchlists. My team and I had done a lot of design revamps to better the experience for our users. We had daily product and design check-ins and had done user researching very frequently. This allowed us to figure out what features needed to be improved and what features we should add for our users. This created our product roadmap and we had seen many improvements in our interactions overtime.
