Background and Philosophies

Anna has an in-depth background in interaction design, information architecture, user experience, and product design. She has been designing for digial experiences for almost 3 years. She is currently a Senior User Experience Designer at Yahoo working as one of the lead designers for the Yahoo Finance experience. She has proficient experience designing for the web, iOS, and android devices.

Anna's current role at Yahoo is lead UX designer for iOS and android finance apps and UX/IXD common core for media organization. UX/IXD common core consists of defining the grid for desktop, tablet, mobile web, navigation systems, and other components that are shareable across engineering and design teams.

Anna's philosophies rely heavily around the ability to creatively solve problems, execute, and design for user intuition and user emotion. Working closely with engineering and user researchers is a crucial factor to the success of the product as well as understanding metrics and data revolving user behavior and user feedback.

Her hobbies include video gaming, researching, reading, learning, and growing. Her interests include health, cancer research, technology, human computer interaction, and emerging products.
